Terms and Conditions

Please be sure to completely read. I will assume as such.

  1. Payment is accepted through PayPal, Venmo, CashApp, and KoFi.
  2. Full payment will be accepted right after the first sketch is sent. Proof of payment must be sent. (Tips are very much accepted!)
  3. I have the right to refuse any commission at any point, and a refund will be issued.
  4. Art may not be used for commercial purposes without my explicit permission. Please discuss with me if you wish to have something created for commercial use.
  5. By buying a commission, you give the right for the art to be used in my portfolio. I will ask whether or not you are okay with me posting online.
  6. All finished pieces will be sent in .png, .jpeg, and .pdf formats.
  7. After a final sketch is sent, refunds will no longer be accepted. If a piece is not completed in the given timeframe, this doesn't apply.
  8. Reference images (moodboards and image boards work) and/or color palettes must be provided to aid in the process.
  9. Revisions must be asked for before the commission is completed. I will send frequent progress photos, so please don't be afraid to point out anything.
  10. If posting the art anywhere, be sure to give explicit credit. Do not crop out or edit out the watermark.
